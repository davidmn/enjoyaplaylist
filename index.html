Enjoy a Playlist

Each week Christopher Macarthur-Boyd and Liam Withnail listen to and talk about/around one of the Rolling Stone's Top 500 Albums.

During the course of the podcast they create a playlist of songs featured in the top 500 but also add tracks that have various levels of relation to the chat.
